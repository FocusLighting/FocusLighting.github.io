About the Designer

Maximilian Brown has worked in the theatrical design world for the last five years, primarily at the Warren Performing Arts Center in Indianapolis. He has also worked at events such as IndyFringe in downtown Indianapolis. He has designed for musicals, ballets, and plays, including Aida (2016), Grease (2012), Our Town (2013), and The Odd Couple (2015). Furthermore, he has been hired as a lighting design consultant for numerous studios. He is currently employed at the Warren Performing Arts Center as the Assistant Technical Director, In-House Master Electrician and In-House Light Designer, where he maintains the standard light plot for the spaces and provides technical support for rental events while also teaching students at the attached Warren Central High School about lights and lighting design.

What We Do

Our full list of services can be found by looking at our Services page, but our Lead Designer (and a team of student designers, if you are willing to allow collaboration from our student staff) are happy to address your customized lighting needs. Whether you are trying to get a standard and universal light plot up for your performance space or simply want a spectacularly dazzling display of lighting prowess at your event, musical, or play, our staff is more than willing to provide. Additionally, we offer technician staff, who will ensure that the designs get run correctly all the time, every time.
